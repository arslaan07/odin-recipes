Lasagna

One of my favorites! Goes great with barbecues and picnics. For a more mild salad, use less green onions.

The first time I rated this, I only gave it a 3, but I've got to change it to a 4. We had it the day I made it and it only tasted like coleslaw with taco seasoning in it, but the next day it was a good bit better and then the day after that, it was really good. So, if you know far enough in advance, make it two days before you'll eat it. It has a great flavor then!

Ingredients

    Steps

    1. 1 cup mayonnaise
    2. 1 tablespoon lime juice
    3. 1 tablespoon ground cumin
    4. 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
    5. 1 teaspoon salt
